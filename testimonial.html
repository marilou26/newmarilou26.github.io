My Empowering Community ALS Implementers-Journey

It is a great opportunity that, we, the implementers of Alternative Learning System have been a part of this ICT training here at Northern Bukidnon Community College. Empowering Community ALS implementers through Digital Citizenship which is essential to the topic of Alternative Learning System.

Started from the first training session that gave us a full of excitement that could bring us more knowledge about technology. I learned and was given a deep understanding about essential components of Computer, creating Microsoft Excel, Powerpoint Presentation wherein I have learned different shorcuts, inputing elements and creating different styles and also creating simple Web Page.

To all the instructors who have provided engaging and informative lessons we have gained which gives us more knowledge to learn that we can teach it with our learners. As the session went through, I gained new knowledge about technology and deepened my understanding of how we can share and practice it to our learners.

we are very grateful for this great experience that having knowledge about this training will lead us to impart knowledge to our learners on Alternative Learning System.
